Our student management module provides a complete solution for managing all aspects of student information and activities.

  • Student attendance tracking and reporting
  • Academic performance monitoring with progress reports
  • Parent-teacher communication portal

  • Health records and medical information management

Efficient Staff Administration System

Our staff management module helps educational institutions efficiently manage their teaching and non-teaching staff.

  • Comprehensive staff profiles and qualification records

  • Staff scheduling and workload distribution

  • Document management for staff certifications
Fee Management

Streamlined Financial Operations

Our fee management module simplifies the entire fee collection process, from setting up fee structures to generating reports.

  • Customizable fee structures for different classes and categories
  • Automated fee collection and receipt generation

  • Fee defaulter tracking and reminders
  • Scholarship and concession management
  • Detailed financial reporting and analytics

Efficient Attendance Management

Our attendance tracking module helps educational institutions efficiently track and manage student and staff attendance.
