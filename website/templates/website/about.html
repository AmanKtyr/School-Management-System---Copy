About Our Company

We're Transforming Education Management in India

Gurukul Setu was founded in 2018 with a mission to revolutionize how educational institutions in India manage their operations. We recognized the challenges faced by schools and colleges in maintaining records, tracking student progress, and managing administrative tasks efficiently.

Our team of education experts and technology professionals came together to create a comprehensive SaaS solution that addresses these challenges, making education management simpler, more efficient, and more effective.
